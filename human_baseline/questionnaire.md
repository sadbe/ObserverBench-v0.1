# Human Baseline Questionnaire

**Instructions for participant:**

Read the Workplace Conflict Dialogue (see `dialogues/workplace_conflict.txt`), then answer the five questions below.

For each question:
1. Circle or write a number from 1 to 10
2. Write **one sentence** explaining your rating

There are no right or wrong answers. We need your honest opinion.
